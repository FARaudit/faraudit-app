# Authority Pack — Source Selection + Pricing
Verified against .gov sources, as-of 2026-06-21. Never invent wage rates or thresholds — use the live lookups.

## Source selection — best-value continuum (FAR 15.3)
- Tradeoff (15.101-1): may award to other than lowest-priced/highest-rated; all factors + relative importance must be stated; can pay a premium for added benefit.
- LPTA (15.101-2): award to the technically ACCEPTABLE proposal with the lowest evaluated price; NO tradeoffs; technical rated acceptable/unacceptable only. [acquisition.gov/far/15.101-1, /15.101-2]

## Evaluation ratings (FAR 15.305 + DoD Source Selection Procedures)
- Evaluate SOLELY on factors/subfactors stated in the solicitation. [acquisition.gov/far/15.305]
- The strength / weakness / significant weakness / deficiency taxonomy + color/adjectival + risk/confidence schemes live in the DoD SSP (not FAR itself). Ratings may be color, adjectival, numeric, or ordinal — documented in the file. Exact color/confidence tables: VERIFY against the live DoD SSP PDF before quoting verbatim.

## Past performance (FAR 42.15 / CPARS)
- One indicator of ability to perform; solicitation must say how offerors with no relevant history are treated.
- CPARS = governmentwide official source. Evaluations at least annually + at completion; contractor gets up to 14 days to comment; disagreements reviewed above the CO. Relevancy + recency applied per the solicitation's criteria.

## Wage determinations (do NOT invent rates)
- Service Contract Act: clause 52.222-41 (+ 42/43) in covered service contracts.
- Davis-Bacon: clause 52.222-6 in covered construction.
- Live lookup = sam.gov/wage-determinations (single source for SCA + DBA WDs). DOL background: dol.gov/agencies/whd.

## TINA — certified cost or pricing data (FAR 15.403-4)
- Current FAR text: $2.5M threshold (awards on/after 2018-07-01). IN FLUX: thresholds inflation-adjust every 5 yrs under FAR 1.109; an adjustment took effect 2025-10-01 (FAR Case 2024-001). 15.403-4 still reads $2.5M as of this check -> treat $2.5M as current but RE-CONFIRM at acquisition.gov/far/15.403-4.

## Cost/price analysis (FAR 15.404)
- Price analysis (15.404-1(b)): preferred = (1) compare competitive proposed prices, (2) compare to previous prices.
- Cost analysis (15.404-1(c)): element-by-element review of cost necessity/reasonableness, indirect/labor rates, CERs.
- FFP usually needs only price analysis; cost-reimbursement REQUIRES a cost realism analysis.
