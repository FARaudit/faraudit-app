NGA is soliciting proposals under RFP HM047626R0039 for the 'Endor' program, which requires on-site film digitization operations, information technology support, and equipment maintenance services at the DIA Headquarters facility in Washington, D.C. The contract will be a single-award FFP (with cost-reimbursable travel CLIN) with a 12-month base period and four 12-month option years. The requiring activity is NGA's Source Foundation GEOINT Operations Office, Dissemination Division, GEOINT Content Exposure Branch (SFPDC), which supports historical imagery digitization for the NSG community.

Primary Objective
Convert historical hardcopy film archives into high-resolution digital imagery (TIFF, GeoTIFF, NITF 2.1 formats) using specialized scanning systems (NextScan Meridian Dragon Apex and Leica DSW700), while maintaining equipment operational readiness and providing IT support, to enable NSG partners worldwide to access scanned historical imagery on demand.
