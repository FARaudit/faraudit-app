Questions

Before you apply.

What counts as an audit?

One full three-call pass on a single solicitation — document classification, CLIN and FAR/DFARS review, and trap detection. Re-running an amended solicitation counts as a new audit.

Can I change plans later?

Yes. Move between Design Partner, Standard and Enterprise at any time. The founding rate stays locked for 12 months from signup even as you add seats.

Where does the data come from?

FARaudit reads directly from SAM.gov, USAspending and FPDS, refreshed continuously. Synopsis scanning runs at 06:15 CT daily across nine defense NAICS codes.

How is my pipeline data secured?

SOC 2 Type II controls with FedRAMP-aligned architecture, encryption at rest and in transit, and role-based access. Your profile and pipeline are never shared across accounts. Request access for the full security brief.
