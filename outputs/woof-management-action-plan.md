## Immediate (this month)

### 1. File IRS Form 8832 — Entity Classification Election

- **Purpose:** Fix mistaken C-Corp filing → elect single-member LLC disregarded-entity treatment
- **Form:** [IRS Form 8832 — Entity Classification Election](https://www.irs.gov/forms-pubs/about-form-8832)
- **Box to check:** Box 1a "Initial classification" → Box 6c "A domestic eligible entity with a single owner electing to be disregarded as a separate entity"
- **Effective date:** Pick a date within (a) 75 days BEFORE the filing date OR (b) 12 months AFTER. For Apex April 2026 stack, pick a backdated effective date around the operating start (commonly 1 Jan 2026 if operations began then).
- **Mailing:** USPS Certified Mail with Return Receipt to the IRS service center for Texas. Confirm current address at irs.gov before sending.
- **Records:** Keep the green return receipt forever — it is your proof of timely filing if challenged.
- **Cost:** $0 (form is free). Pay roughly $45 for certified-mail handling.
