Most defense contractors read a solicitation and never stop to ask what kind of document they are actually reading.

There are three types. Each requires a completely different proposal strategy.

SOW — Statement of Work. Government tells you exactly what to do. Your job: comply precisely with every stated task. Compliance-first.

PWS — Performance Work Statement. Government tells you the outcome they want. Your job: propose HOW you achieve it. Innovation opportunity. Outcome-first.

SOO — Statement of Objectives. Government describes the problem. Your job: write your own Performance Work Statement. Maximum latitude.

Most contractors treat every solicitation like a SOW.

That is why they score below expectation on PWS and SOO awards.

FARaudit identifies the document type as the first line of every audit. Every recommendation that follows — bid strategy, proposal structure, evaluation factor response — starts from that classification.
